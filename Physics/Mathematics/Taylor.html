The Maclaurin & Taylor Expansions

Many functions can be represented using a power series. Examples are: \[\frac{1}{1-x}=1+x+x^2+x^3+...\]
\[f(x)=f(0)+xf'(0)+\frac{1}{2!}x^{2}f''(0)+\frac{1}{3!}x^3f'''(0)+...\] \[f(x)=f(x_0)+(x-x_0)f'(x_0)+\frac{(x-x_0)^2}{2!}f''(x_0)+\frac{(x-x_0)^3}{3!}f'''(x_0)+...\]
