Popcorn

Group of moviegoers. Half we given bucket of popcorn, other half given extra-large bucket
of popcorn. For these two groups, half were given fresh popcorn, other half given 14-day old
popcorn (later rated 2/10).

However, despite the differences in taste, all those with the extra-large bucket ate about 40%
more than those with the regular bucket.

In a rational world the only things that effect our decision to eat is hunger and enjoyment.
Seeing as crappy popcorn doesnt fufill these we can assume the decision to eat more is a
irrational decision. The best way to go on a diet is the make your plate smaller.
