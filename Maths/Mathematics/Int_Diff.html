Intergration and Differentation

Differentation

Polynomial

\[\frac{d}{dx}\,x^n=nx^{n-1}\]

Exponential

\[\frac{d}{dx}\,e^{f(x)}=f'(x)e^{f(x)}\]

Product Rule

\[\frac{d}{dx}\,f(x)g(x)=f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)\]

Quotient Rule

\[\frac{d}{dx}\,f(g(x))=\frac{df(x)}{dg(x)}\times \frac{df(x)}{dx}\]

Chain Rule

\[\frac{d}{dx}\,\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{g^2(x)}\]
